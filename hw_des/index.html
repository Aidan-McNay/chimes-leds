Hardware Design

Our system was composed of three types of board:

  • A controller board (located at the main playing level, acting as the primary user interface)

  • A sensor board (located in the belfry for collecting sensor measurements)

  • Four face boards (located in each clock face for driving the LEDs)

All of these boards communicate over a shared CAN bus for reliable communication of information

LED Setup

The clocktower currently uses strips of RGBW LEDs to light the clock faces. These LEDs have 5 pins: +12V, R, G, B, and W. We suspected this meant that the non-power lines should PWM’d to ground to adjust the amount of that color; after probing these lines, we confirmed that this was the case, with the model below showing the strips in beige (anticipating connecting them to some form of switch)
